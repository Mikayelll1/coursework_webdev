Welcome to Blood Charity Organization



This is the home page for Blood, our Organization helping those who are hungry.



Our Mission:



Our mission here is to contribute to the betterment of the world by stribing to provide opportunities for those in need. We are comitted to allocated 40% of our profits towards supporting children in Africa, with the goal of alleviating hunger and fostering sustatinable development.



In our line of work, sometimes it is hard to help everyone, but we try our best, and with your help we can make zero hunger a dream that can come true. Around the globe, billions are starving and fighting off hunger, and this is an obvious issue in Africa, but it still happens around the globe, there are products in our store that you can purchase, of which, 75% of profit from those items will go directly to funding missions to send food and build taps in places where there are struggles for nutrition, together we will be able to make zero hunger and dream that can come true.         



Above you will find links to our pages where you can meet the team members working on this project, as well as the links to our store, where you can purchase products to help those in need, a feedback page so you can show support and give us advice on what we can improve, if you wish to do so, we also have a gallery where you can take a look at the images of the people we have helped so far. In addition to this we also have Content Pages relaying information on what we wish to do , as well as views of the Page Editors; the remarkable people who helped put all this together.
